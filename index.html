About me

I received my Ph.D. from Department of Electrical Engineering at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2019, supervised by with Prof. Shu-hung Leung and Prof. Taejoon Kim. Before this, I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China, in 2013 and 2015, respectively.

Research interests

My researchs focus on low-rank matrix recovery and its application in millimeter wave channel estimation. The current research topics include:

  • Millimeter wave channel estimation: AoAs and AoDs estimation, hybrid precoding, and subspace estimation

  • Low-rank matrix recovery: restricted isolated property analysis and convex optimization

  • Statistical analysis: probability analysis and robust parameter estimation
